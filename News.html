News About GM Computer Lab

Limpopo's rural youngsters will now have access to the modern computer lab that will provide them with the tools they need to become proficient in digital technology, it will also help them to apply at universities after completing their matric. The purpose of establishing GM Computer Lab is to make lives of our youngsters easier helping students learn, work with software programs, complete assignments. This Computer Lab will make a better future for our youngsters. Our computer lab aims to improve learners technical skills for the future.
